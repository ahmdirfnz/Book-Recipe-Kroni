Delicious Omelette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Filling options (e.g., cheese, vegetables, ham)

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Step 2: Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted.
  3. Step 3: Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it set slightly at the edges.
  4. Step 4: With a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow underneath.
  5. Step 5: When the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add your desired fillings on one half of the omelette.
  6. Step 6: Fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape.
  7. Step 7: Cook for an additional minute or until the omelette is cooked through but still moist.
