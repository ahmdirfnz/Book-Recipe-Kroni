Delicious Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Step 2: In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Step 3: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Step 4: Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Step 5: Pour 1/4 cup portions of batter onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  6. Step 6: Repeat until all the batter is used.
